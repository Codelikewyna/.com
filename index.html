Development Process From Zero To Hero

1

Get ReadyRequirements Analysis

First, figure out what the project needs, who it's for, what content it will have, and the features it should include.

2

Plan the LookContent and Design

Decide how the content should be organized, what details each part should including buttons, menus, and how easy it is to use.

3

Build ItDevelopment

Set up the tools needed to create the project, put in all the details, and organize everything properly.

4

Get Ready to ShowPre-launch

Before showing it to everyone, I create a special space SPECIALLY FOR YOU to test and make sure everything works as it should.

5

Show It to the WorldLaunch

Once everything is okay, share it with everyone by putting it out there, making sure databases are set up, and storing information properly.

6

Keep It GoingMaintainance

After it is out there, take care of it by fixing any issues and making improvements to keep it running smoothly.
